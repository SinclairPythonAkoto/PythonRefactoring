Rate This Property!

Description

This website is designed to allow anyone to rate the condotion of a property - no matter if you're a tenant, neighbour or just passing by.
As a tenant you can review the condition of the property you're renting, or even your tenants.
Neighbours can review the property also, along with anyone passing by - it doesn't have to be all negative!

Factors

Here are some factors to consider when creating a review - some factors can also be positive!

Parties & Large Gatherings

Parties and gatherings causing a disturbance.

Rudness

Rudeness or harrasment from people within the property.

Fighting

Fighting or aggressive behaviour.


Domestic Disturbances

Neighbours arguing or being disruptive.

Dirty / Unkempt

Piles of rubbish & litter left laying around the property.

Drug Misuse

Drugs are used in or around the property.


Broken Furniture

Furniture broken before or during moving into property.

Mould

Damp and/or mould within the property.

Broken Appliances

Broken appliances within the property making it difficult to live in - broken fridge, boiler, cooker etc


Infestation

Rats, rodents, bugs and/or bugs cohibiting in the property.

Messy Housemates / Flatmates

Unkepmt house mates making it difficult to live iwht them..

Difficult Landlords

Landlords being diificult, causing harrasment, increasing rent without formal notice.
